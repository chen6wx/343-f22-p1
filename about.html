About Me.

I have always loved and enjoyed the arts whether it is painting, sculpture or photography, there is nothing like the capturing and living in precious memories.
I always wanted to share my art with others, but as a young person it was difficult to get started in the arts. With the prevalence of social media, sharing your art is easier than ever before. I love finding inspiration in the mundane and think that through my photography I can show others how beautiful ordinary things can be.
Exploring the beauty through lens, painting and words. Searching for what matters.
"I will always pursue, unfettered by a deadline and driven by curiosity. I seek a kind of beauty that encompasses both the light and shadows."
